    • Free peer tutoring, counselling on time management and study skills.
    • Spaces for group and individual study.

    • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:30 - 5pm
    • Contact: 803-323-3929

  • House Calls
    • Request a tutoring session held in the residence halls in a variety of courses.
    • Tutoring begins after midterms.
    • Hours: By appointment, Sundays-Thursdays, 7pm - 10pm

    • Tutoring available for several of the core business courses.
    • Location: Thurmond 107

My Money Matters

  • First Year Finances

    Budgeting Tips

    • Take a few minutes to map out your daily and weekly expenses
    • Ask yourself, "How much money do I have over this period"
    • Plan your spending accordingly.
    • Give yourself a buffer of funds.
    • If you have a plan, you're more likely to meet your goal.
